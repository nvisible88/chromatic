The Science

Adapted from the Farnsworth-Munsell 100 Hue Test, the clinical gold standard for color vision used by ophthalmologists, design schools, paint manufacturers, and quality-control labs. The test isolates three independent dimensions of chromatic perception — hue, saturation, and lightness — to produce a profile of how your visual system processes color.
